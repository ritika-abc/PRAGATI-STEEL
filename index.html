Our Products

Maize (Corn)

We supply high-quality Maize that is carefully sourced from reliable producers and processed to ensure cleanliness, freshness, and nutritional value. Our maize is suitable for:

  • Animal Feed (Poultry, Cattle, Aqua, etc.)
  • Food Processing Industry
  • Starch and Biofuel Production
  • Export and Wholesale Distribution

Available Forms: Whole Maize, Broken Maize, Yellow Maize
Packaging: 25kg / 50kg PP Bags or as per customer requirements

Stable Bleaching Powder

Our Stable Bleaching Powder is a high-purity calcium hypochlorite-based compound, widely used for disinfection and sanitation. Known for its excellent shelf life and high chlorine content, it is suitable for:

  • Water Treatment Plants
  • Swimming Pools
  • Sanitation in Public Health & Municipal Applications
  • Industrial Cleaning & Disinfection

Chlorine Content: 32% - 34%
Packaging: 25kg HDPE Drums / Customized Packaging
