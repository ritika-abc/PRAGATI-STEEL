Who We Are —

PRAGATI STEEL

With over 15 years of hands-on industry experience.

Established in 2022, PRAGATI STEEL is a trusted name in the Indian manufacturing sector, specializing in high-quality door locks, cupboard locks, hinges, tower bolts, and a wide range of precision sheet metal components. With over 15 years of industry experience, our founder Mr. Soubhik De, a B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering, brings technical expertise and leadership to our operations.
